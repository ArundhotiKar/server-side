PawMart is a one-stop online platform for pet lovers, offering a wide range of products and services for pets.

## Features

- **Pet Adoption:** Find and adopt pets in need of a loving home.  
- **Pet Food:** Browse and purchase high-quality food for your pets.  
- **Accessories:** Explore a variety of accessories including toys, collars, and more.  
- **User-Friendly Interface:** Easy navigation with a clean, responsive design.  
- **Secure Checkout:** Safe and reliable payment options for online orders.  
- **Interactive Search & Filters:** Quickly find products or pets using search and filter features.  

## Tech Stack

- React.js  
- Tailwind CSS  
- Firebase Authentication & Database  
- Netlify (Deployment)
